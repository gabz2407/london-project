Why London?

Endless Excitement Awaits in London

London is a huge city that is filled with landmarks, world-class museums, entertainment venues, and tourist attractions. So trying to see the best of London in a day is not an easy task, especially if you want to make the most out of your time, but also keep it enjoyable and fun.

My Favourite Restaurants in London

SMOKESTACK

A buzzing barbecue restaurant with an open kitchen and an industrial feel. Highlights include the brisket and ribs: these are brined, oak-smoked, coated with a sweet and sour BBQ sauce and chargrilled - the results being unctuous and incredibly satisfying.

Address:

35 Sclater St, London E1 6LB England
What I like about it

I am suspicious to say what I like the most abous this restaurant because I am a meat lover but, despite that, I love the ambience and the staff, plus the deserts... OMG... amazing!

Volare

Founded in 2015 by two Spanish sisters inspired by the love and passion of their Neapoletan Great-grandfather who opened his own pizzeria in Argentina in the 1920s. This menu is a fusion of our heritage and knowledge of Spanish and Italian cuisines.

Address:

167 Bowes Road, London N11 2JA England
What I like about it

This is the best place for pizza and pasta I have ever been to. Food is make when you order to, so if you order some starters they will wait for you to finish and ask you if they can make your main dishes. Exquisite!

Mosaic

Mosaic is a convivial city all-day dining venue. Boasting a beautiful open kitchen and City-facing views, Mosaic is the perfect place to gather for business meetings, long lunches or to unwind with a glass of Taittinger Champagne after a visit to Shakespeare's Globe.

Address:

60 Upper Thames Street, London EC4V 3AD
What I like about it

Either you are a vegetarian or not this restaurant is for you! This luxurios bistro restaurant is located in the Westin Hotel. The service is amazing and the staff could not be more polite and helpful.
